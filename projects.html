Projects

The Projects section highlights ongoing creative work across 3D modelling, fantasy world‑building, digital sculpting, and visual storytelling. Each project reflects a commitment to growth, experimentation, and the pursuit of lifelike detail and emotional depth.

Phoenix V2 — Relief Sculpt

A dramatic, relief‑style 3D sculpt designed for printability and strong lighting contrast. This piece explores themes of rebirth, resilience, and transformation — core ideas behind Crown Creatives.

Current focus: refining feather detail, improving depth layering, and preparing the model for resin printing.

Emerald Crown — Symbol & Identity

The Emerald Crown is a recurring symbol throughout the Crown Creatives brand. It represents clarity, strength, and the ongoing journey of rebuilding life with intention. This project includes logo development, colour studies, and animated effects used across the site.
